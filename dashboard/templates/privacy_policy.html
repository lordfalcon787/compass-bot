PRIVACY POLICY
Last Updated: 03/20/2025
1. INTRODUCTION
This Privacy Policy describes how Compass ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information obtained from users ("you") of the Compass Discord bot ("Compass"). By using Compass, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, you must discontinue use of Compass immediately.
2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
When you use Compass, we may collect the following types of information:
  • User Data: Discord user IDs, server IDs, and usernames as necessary for bot functionality.
  • Message Data: Content, embeds, buttons, etc for bot functionality.
  • Command Usage Data: Logs of commands executed to improve service quality and functionality.
  • Server Information: Configuration data, such as settings and preferences related to Compass's features.
  • Form Submissions: Any data submitted through forms (modals) within Compass is stored.
  • Other Voluntarily Provided Information: Any data you choose to provide while interacting with Compass.
3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
We may use the collected data for the following purposes:
  • To operate and maintain Compass's features.
  • To improve and optimize user experience.
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and prevent abuse.
  • To provide necessary customer support.
  • To analyze and monitor bot performance and usage trends.
4. DATA STORAGE AND RETENTION
  • All collected data, including form submissions, may be stored in an external MongoDB database.
  • No personal information is stored.
  • Data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill its purpose or comply with legal obligations.
  • If you wish to request data deletion, you may contact us through official channels.
5. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE
We do not sell, trade, or share user data with any third parties. However, we may disclose information under the following circumstances:
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, court order, or regulatory authority.
  • Security & Enforcement: To protect our rights, investigate violations, or prevent fraud and abuse.
  • Service Providers: In limited cases, data may be shared with third-party services necessary for bot operation (e.g., hosting providers), excluding personal information.
6. SECURITY MEASURES
We take reasonable precautions to protect user data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
7. THIRD-PARTY LINKS AND SERVICES
Compass may contain links or integrations with third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external services. Please review their respective policies before use.
8. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY
We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted with the "Last Updated" date revised accordingly. Continued use of Compass after modifications constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
9. CONTACT INFORMATION
If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through official channels (lordfalcon787@gmail.com).
